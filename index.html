Hello!
I am currently a research assistant in Natural Language Processing and Speech Processing at Qatar Computing Research Institute in Qatar, in QCRI-ALT group. I am supervised by Dr. Ahmed Ali and Dr. Shammur Absar Chowdhury. As of Fall 2024 I am looking for PhD Opportunities in Natural Language Processing or Speech Processing.

My current research focus is Arabic centric LLM, Automatic Speech Recognition.

I am passionate about machine learning, convinced that we are going through an AI revolution, and have been in this area since 2019. I obtained a MSc in Machine Learning from KTH Royal Institute of Technology Department of Computer Science and Electical Engineering and a MEng from Telecom Paris (a leading French School) in Sep 2023.
